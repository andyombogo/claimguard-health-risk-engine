# Data Dictionary

All files in this repository are synthetic and intended only for prototype development, testing, and demonstration. The primary dataset for the ClaimGuard demo is generated by `src/data_processing/generate_synthetic_claims.py` and saved to `data/synthetic/synthetic_health_claims.csv`.

## Dataset Notes

- Default generated dataset size: `560` claims
- Random seed: `20260428`
- IDs, provider names, diagnoses, procedures, and workflows are synthetic and generic
- Date fields are logically ordered and may be blank when not applicable
- `approved_amount` is never greater than `claim_amount`; pending and under-review claims may have `0`

## Intentional Risk Patterns Included

- Duplicate and near-duplicate claims with matching member, provider, diagnosis, and closely timed billing
- Abnormal billing cases where claim amounts are several multiples above the median for similar diagnosis-procedure pairs
- Diagnosis-treatment mismatches created by swapping in clinically inconsistent procedure codes
- Missing-document scenarios for invoice, discharge summary, and lab report checks
- Provider concentration patterns with higher claim volume and elevated billing among selected synthetic providers
- Member concentration patterns with repeated claims inside a 90-day lookback window
